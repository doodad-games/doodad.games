Privacy Policy

Some of our games may collect user data to deliver personalised ads, or to allow us to understand how users interact with our games so we can best improve them.

Collection of data is performed via Unity Ads and Unity Analytics. This is their privacy policy.

Information regarding the data collected for each of our games is listed below.

Bombardier

  • Standard Unity Analytics data. (No ads)
  • To help us understand how our game is being played, information when the following in-game events occur:
    • When a new game is started.
    • When a game has ended, along with the following metadata:
      • Game duration.
      • Score.
      • Cause of the game's end.

Viking Trickshot

  • Standard Unity Analytics data. (No ads)
  • To help us understand how our game is being played, information when the following in-game events occur:
    • When a match has started, and whether that match is 1 or 2 player, and local or online.
    • When an online matchmaking event occurs, and whether:
      • that was to enter the queue, be matched with a player, or start the session (both players ready).
      • that was matchmaking with a random player, or a private session.

Alphabet Earthquake; I'm a Fish (iOS); Item TD 2; Rabbit Flick; Stone TD; Typing Gladiators; this website

No data is collected or shared at all.

FrankenStorm TD: Prologue; I'm a Fish (Android); Neanderfall; Reef Defenders; Space Trash; Two Tweets; uniTower; Villager
